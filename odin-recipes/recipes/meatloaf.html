Meatloaf

Description

This meatloaf recipe doesn't take long to make at all, and it's very good! This recipe includes bison rather than beef and you won't know the difference.

Crushed crackers may be used in place of bread crumbs, if preferred. This is a quick meal to prepare, can be done in 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of pepper

For the sauce

  • 1 cup of ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon mustard

How to Make a Meatloaf at Home

  1. Mix the loaf ingredients, then transfer to a loaf pan.
  2. Mix the sauce ingredients, then pour over the loaf.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for an hour or until the meatloaf is no longer pink.
